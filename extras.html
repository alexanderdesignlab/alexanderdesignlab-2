About Me

I have always been drawn to the way visuals could make something feel clearer and more enjoyable, even before I began my study of digital design. As a child in school I loved harnessing my creativity to take something messy and turn it into something that made sense.

Digital design allows me to take that instinct a step further. Now I get to explore how color, spacing, motion, and structure can shape an experience. I love the process of making something that not only looks good, but actually works better because of the way it’s designed. As a designer, I utilize my skills to illustrate brand identity, communicate information concisely, and leave a positive impression on my audience.

Currently, I am studying Digital Design at Mountainland Technical College, improving and expanding my skillset every day. I use my proficiency in the Adobe Suite to create dynamic visuals for a diverse range of objectives.
